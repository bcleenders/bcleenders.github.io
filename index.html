Bramspr compiler

Together with Jasper Driessens, I wrote a compiler using ANTLR and ASM. It compiles the Bramspr language, which Jasper and I developed from scratch, and supports functions, enumerations, structs, arrays(partially) and more... Because we compile to JBC, our compiled Bramspr code is fully cross-platform.

Trend and Topic Detection in Hacker News

During one of my courses at TU Berlin, Marc Romeyn and I performed data analysis on a crawl of Hacker News.

We used two machine learning algorithms (LDA and Word2Vec + k-means) to cluster articles based on topics and looked at the popularity of these topics over time.
